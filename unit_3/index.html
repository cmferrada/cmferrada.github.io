a weekend in:

SANTA BARBARA, CALIFORNIA

Santa Barbara is the perfect weekend destination. It combines the relaxation of a seaside getaway combined with the delicious bounty of wine country. On top of the ocean breezes and endless tastings of wine, bonus perks include the young, artistic energy, the top notch restaurant scene, and the fact that the main scene is crazy easy to get around via bike-riding and walking. Jack and I went for Valentine's Day weekend but regardless of when you go, you're likely to fall in love.

who are we?

We are two 20-something professionals living in LA with Monday-Friday jobs and next to no vacation time. We love to travel, explore, and eat—and we prefer to do that outside the confines of home. With limited free time and funds, we’ve learned to scratch our travelling itch by embracing the magic that is the weekend. Because life is too short.
