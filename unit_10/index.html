a weekend in:

SANTA BARBARA, CALIFORNIA

Santa Barbara is the perfect weekend destination. It combines the relaxation of a seaside getaway combined with the delicious bounty of wine country. On top of the ocean breezes and endless tastings of wine, bonus perks include the young, artistic energy, the top notch restaurant scene, and the fact that all of the action is crazy easy to get around via bike-riding and walking. Jack and I went for Valentine's Day weekend but regardless of when you go, you're likely to fall in love.
